📱 Full collection of TikTok emojis

TikTok is a social network and video hosting service created in 2018 by ByteDance, a Beijing-based company. It is designed to create and 👀 View short videos.

TikTok is used by people to watch entertainment content, but expert videos can also be found in it. The content of an application is as diverse as its target audience varies: from instructions on how to choose the right 🥑 Avocados to fast psychological 📝 Tests. People use TikTok to exchange emotions, 🤣 Funny videos, and also to teach 💃 Dancing, life hacks, etc.

The service has both a built-in emoji system available on other devices and platforms and its own set.

Use emojis in TikTok to express your ❤️ Opinion and 😂 Emotions in the comments after watching the video, to ➡️ Share your favorite videos with 🤝 Friends to, ➕ Add or to create an emoji clip as a title or a short description.

Interesting fact: One of the popular emoticons on TikTok is the 🤡 Clown emoji. It means a mockery of Tiktoker, as well as the author’s self-irony about his work. Also, such emojis as: ❤️ Red heart, 🔥 Fire, 😂 Face with tears of joy and 👍 Thumb up are popular on the TikTok app.

No less popular is the ✍️ Writing hand emoji, which is used to express sarcastic observation.

How to add standard emojis to comments:

  1. Open the desired video in recommendations or subscriptions.
  2. Click on the comments on the right and click on the emoji icon in the appeared window with a text box.
  3. Select the desired emoticon from the emoji block and click on it 1 time.
  4. Click once on the plane icon to send a comment.

To use custom emojis from TikTok, you need to enter a specific shortcode, when you submit it, it will automatically be replaced with an emoticon.

How to add secret emojis in comments and videos:

  1. Open the desires video in recommendations or subscriptions.
  2. Click on the comments on the right and enter a specific shortcode for the desired emoji in the text box that appears. For example, to send a crying emoticon, you need to enter the [cry] shortcode.
  3. Click once on the plane icon to send a comment.
