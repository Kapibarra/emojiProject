How to create a custom emoji with Emoji Kitchen?

There are two ways to do this: using the function of the same name in the beta version of Gboard and through the Emoji Kitchen website.

To create a custom emoji using the Emoji Kitchen feature in the beta version of Gboard, you must:

    1. Go to the dialog box in the desired application and open the Gboard keyboard;
    2. Click on the selected emoji and choose the option you like from the combinations that appear;
    3. Click on it and it will automatically go as a sticker.

To create a custom emoji through the Emoji Kitchen website, you must:

    1. Go to the Emoji Kitchen website;
    2. Click on the first square, select the desired emoji from the list provided and click on it. Then repeat this action with the second square;
    3. A new emoji will appear automatically, you can also share it, save it to your device, and also click on the update button and see what options for this combination are still possible;
    4. Also, you can click on the random button and the site will give you a random combination of emoji.
